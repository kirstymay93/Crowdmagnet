CrowdMagnet AI 🚀

CrowdMagnet AI is an AI-powered marketing assistant designed to help event organisers build real audiences, increase engagement, and sell more tickets through viral marketing campaigns and data-driven growth strategies.

⸻

🎯 Event

Island Stampede – Tasmania’s Premier Professional Bull Riding Event

Goal

5,000 Attendees

⸻

✨ Features

Generate professional marketing content in seconds, including:

* 🔥 Viral Campaign Ideas
* 📱 Social Media Posts
* 🎥 Short-Form Video Ideas (TikTok, Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts)
* 🎁 Giveaway Campaigns
* 📊 Audience Growth Plans
* 📅 Marketing Calendars
* 📧 Email Campaigns
* 🎟 Ticket Sales Strategies
* 🤝 Sponsorship Promotion Ideas
* 📣 Community Engagement Campaigns

⸻

🚀 How It Works

1. Enter your event details.
2. Set your attendance goal.
3. Select the marketing assets you want to generate.
4. Let CrowdMagnet AI create a customised campaign to help grow your audience.
